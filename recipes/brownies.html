Choclate Brownie Recipe

Who doesn't like chocolate brownies? This recipe sure will impress all of your friends!

Just follow along this simple receipe and all your friends will love your brownies!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Melt the butter with sugar, salt, cocoa powder
  2. Add the Vanilla and eggs
  3. Add the flour
  4. Bake brownies for 25 minutes
