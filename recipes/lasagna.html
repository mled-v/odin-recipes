Lasagna Recipe

Real italian beef lasagna recipe

This is an easy beef lasagna recipe. There is no need to boil noodles or work on this for hours!

This is the perfect weekend lazy meal. Just follow along these few easy steps to have perfect beef lasagna!

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Seasoning
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 F
  2. Brown the beef in a pan
  3. Prepare the sauces
  4. Stack the lasagna
